Travel Tips

Before you pack your bags and head off on an awesome adventure overseas, the most critical (yet boring) step is to plan things out. I know, I know – researching places to stay, figuring out a budget, and designing an itinerary sounds about as fun as joining a company meeting on Friday at 5 PM.

But putting in that work ahead of time is what takes a trip from chaotic, stressful, and messy to smooth, affordable, and unforgettable (in a good way). Speaking from plenty of travel mishaps in our years of nomading around the globe, believe me when I say that solid trip planning pays off big time. Source material
